Accessibility Features

We are committed to ensuring our website is accessible to everyone.

Alt Text for Images

All images on our site include descriptive alt text to aid screen readers. This ensures that visually impaired users can understand the content of images.

Keyboard Navigation

Our website is designed to be navigable using a keyboard. Users can easily access all sections without relying on a mouse.

Readable Fonts and Colors

We use high-contrast colors and readable font sizes to ensure that our content is accessible to users with visual impairments. Text can be resized without loss of content or functionality.

ARIA Labels

We implement Accessible Rich Internet Applications (ARIA) labels for enhanced accessibility, providing additional context to assistive technologies.
